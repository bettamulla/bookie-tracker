Understanding Matched Betting

Matched betting is a risk-free method to profit from bookmaker promotions by covering all outcomes of an event.

Key Concepts:

  • Back Bet: Betting for something to happen (placed with a bookmaker)
  • Lay Bet: Betting against something happening (placed on a betting exchange)
  • Qualifying Bet: Initial bet to unlock a promotion
  • Free Bet: Risk-free bet obtained through a promotion

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Find an Offer: Identify a profitable bookmaker promotion
  2. Calculate Stakes: Use the calculator to determine back and lay stakes
  3. Place Your Bets: Place the back bet with the bookmaker and the lay bet on the exchange
  4. Record the Bet: Add the bet to your tracker with all details
  5. Update Status: Mark the bet as won/lost once the event concludes
  6. Repeat: Move on to the next profitable offer

Pro Tips for Success

  • Start with simple offers like "Bet £10 Get £30" before moving to more complex promotions
  • Always read the terms and conditions of each offer carefully
  • Keep separate bankrolls for bookmakers and betting exchanges
  • Track every bet to identify your most profitable strategies
  • Be organized and methodical - matched betting rewards consistency
